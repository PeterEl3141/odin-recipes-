Spaghetti Bolognese!

This hearty Italian classic is perfect for any time of year, and can be turned into a chilli con carne on reheating with the addition of just a few ingredients!!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1/2 cup dry red wine (sub water or beef broth/stock)
  • 2 beef bouillon cubes
  • 800g can crushed tomato
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp white sugar
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper

  • Method

    1. Add the oil to a pan at medium heat and fry the beef mince until browned. Then add the onions and garlic and fry for a further 2 minutes.
    2. Add the stock, wine, chopped tomatoes, and tomato paste. Stir, and allow the flavors to infuse for a further 10-15 minutes.
    3. Season with salt pepper, bay leaves and thyme. Feel free to experiment with other herbs such as oreganno, and rosemary.
    4. Serve with pasta. Consider adding (lots of) Cheese!
