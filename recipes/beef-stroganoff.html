Beef Stroganoff

Can you imagine a better 'winter warmer' than tender chunks of beef falling apart into a creamy sauce, topped with cheese? Nor can we!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 250g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tbsp plain flour
  • 500g fillet steak, sliced
  • 150g crème fraîche
  • 1 tsp English mustard
  • 100ml beef stock
  • ½ small pack of parsley, chopped
